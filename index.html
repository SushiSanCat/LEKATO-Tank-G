Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update the firmware?

Connect the pedal to your computer via USB and use the official LEKATO software to update the firmware.

Can I load my own IRs?

Yes, you can load up to 20 custom IRs using the USB connection and the LEKATO editor.

Is the pedal suitable for bass guitar?

Yes, the Tank G works well with both electric and bass guitars.

Does it support stereo output?

No, the Tank G provides mono output only.

How do I save and recall presets?

Use the pedal's interface to save your custom tones to any of the 128 user preset slots. Recall them instantly by selecting the desired slot.

Can I use third-party IRs?

Yes, you can import third-party IR files in WAV format using the LEKATO editor software.

What is the warranty period?

The LEKATO Tank G comes with a standard 1-year warranty. Please check with your retailer for details.

Does it support expression pedals?

Yes, you can connect an expression pedal for real-time control of effects parameters.
